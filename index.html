E - COMMERCE

E-commerce (electronic commerce) is the activity of electronically buying or selling of products on online services or over the Internet. E-commerce draws on technologies such as mobile commerce, electronic funds transfer, supply chain management, Internet marketing, online transaction processing, electronic data interchange (EDI), inventory management systems, and automated data collection systems. E-commerce is in turn driven by the technological advances of the semiconductor industry, and is the largest sector of the electronics industry.

BENIFITS OF E-COMMERCE

The benefits of e-commerce include no geographical limitations, lower costs, comparison shopping, quick location of products, open availability and improved markets for niche products. Also, no need exists for a physical location, which eliminates costs associated with operations and overhead.

DISADVANTAGES OF E-COMMERCE

Different people sing to different tunes. The demise of the personal touch in online transactions can be the biggest disadvantage of e-commerce. Unless you are using a website to order a pizza, e-commerce website delivery takes a lot longer to get the goods into your hands
